Creative Direction

I use facilitation, coaching, data, and strategic planning to help sharpen different points of view into consensus and then work to help folks unleash their collective creativity together. I believe the best directors are servant-leaders first, and I'm a tireless advocate for the brilliant people whose creative work brings to life the stories we're working together to tell.

Multimedia Production

For over twenty years, I have been creating music, movies, illustrations, animations, software—and everything in between—to tell emotionally engaging stories. I thrive within constraints and seek out opportunities to create the things that others believe can't be done. I'm a self-taught expert at making mistakes, but I'm even better at learning from them.
